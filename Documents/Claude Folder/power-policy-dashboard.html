Collateral Requirements

  • Letters of credit (bank rated A-/A3+)
  • Cash deposits
  • Corporate/parental guaranty (issuer rated BBB-/Baa3+)

  • Posted before interconnecting facilities constructed
  • Refundable once project placed in service

  • Study fee: $100K–$300K depending on project size
  • Intermediate Agreement required before ERCOT study begins
  • Site control proof: deed, signed lease (min 5-yr term), or purchase option
  • Must disclose backup generation, duplicative requests
  • Remote disconnect capability required (post-Dec 2025)
  • EEA3 curtailment: ERCOT can direct backup gen or curtailment
  • $47.5B capital plan (2026-2030)
